MARKETING TRENDS YOU CAN'T MISS IN 2023

Marketing is considered a continuous flow throughout the evolution of the 4.0 technology era today. A marketing trend that is booming now can become outdated within just a few months—this is common in the market. Keeping up with the latest trends will bring great opportunities for your business to grow and stand out among thousands of competitors. Here are some marketing trends predicted to become popular in 2023 that you definitely cannot overlook.

1. Short-form Video

Short-form videos typically last between 60 seconds to 2 minutes. However, depending on the platform, the video length can extend up to 10 minutes (TikTok, YouTube Shorts, etc.). In today’s fast-paced and busy life, people tend to pay more attention to short videos rather than longer clips.
According to HubSpot surveys, up to 90% of marketers are currently using this format for their marketing activities, and it also has the highest ROI. Additionally, 21% of marketers are preparing to experiment with short-form videos in 2023.

2. Influencer Marketing

Influencer marketing has been one of the most focused trends in recent years. Reports indicate that one in four marketers are using this trend, and it delivers the second-highest ROI compared to other marketing activities.
HubSpot forecasts a significant growth in influencer marketing in 2023, with 17% of marketers planning to invest in this method.
Another consumer trend survey shows that 33% of Gen Z purchased products based on influencer recommendations rather than suggestions from friends or family.
The duo of short-form video and influencer marketing will be a perfect combination to optimize and enhance your business’s ROI.

3. SEO Maintains Its Position

One of the effective and timeless communication trends businesses can consider in 2023 is search engine optimization (SEO) for Google keyword ranking.
29% of marketers believe that having an effective SEO strategy is a powerful tool that helps businesses gain clear recognition in the market and increase conversion rates.

4. Brand Personality

In 2022, it was common to see brands personified in vivid, viral ways on social media channels. This is a positive sign for Vietnam’s marketing market as it integrates with global trends.
According to statistics, 16% of marketers said they will experiment with brand personification in 2023. Among them, 89% who have invested in this form plan to maintain or increase their budgets.
This trend is very effective and beneficial for businesses. However, caution is needed because it is a “double-edged sword”: choosing the wrong message or brand personality may seriously affect the brand image and recognition being built.

5. Direct Message

Direct Message marketing is a system where businesses communicate directly with customers through messages to create interaction, exchange information, and reach agreements on commercial transactions.
Though relatively new, HubSpot reports that 29% of marketers are utilizing this trend across communication channels, and 87% of them will continue investing in Direct Message to better reach and serve customers.
Another survey shows that over one-fifth of Gen Z and nearly one-fourth of Millennials have proactively contacted businesses via social media to experience attentive customer service.
This communication marketing trend ranks third in ROI, just after short-form video and influencer marketing, making it a tool few businesses can afford to ignore. When used properly to build a professional communication channel combined with excellent customer care, businesses can achieve the expected conversion rates and profits.
Above are marketing trends expected to go viral in 2023. As a professional marketer, you should definitely not miss these and should combine and leverage the strengths of each trend. This can help your business secure a sustainable position in the hearts of customers amid countless competitors today.
Source: HubSpot
