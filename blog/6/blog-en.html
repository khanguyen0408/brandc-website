WHAT IF FAMOUS BRANDS WERE PERSONIFIED?

Charming, bold, rebellious, or classy… these are all adjectives used to describe human personalities. But did you know these words can also describe the personality of a brand? So, what is brand personality? And how does it benefit your business?

Definition

Brand personality is the collection of visual associations, memories, characteristics, and values that a brand imprints on and leaves in the minds of customers. This is what helps consumers recognize and differentiate a brand among thousands of competitors.

The value it brings to a brand

Brand personality plays a crucial role in building customer affection and loyalty toward a business. The more distinctive and outstanding the personality, the easier it is to build customer loyalty. As a result, brand recognition becomes more prominent and better positioned in today’s fiercely competitive market.
