MARKETING TRENDS IN 2023 (PART 2)

2023 is not only the year of groundbreaking technologies but also a year of changing approaches and building customer relationships. Marketing trends have gradually become key factors, opening great opportunities for businesses. In this article, we will continue to explore marketing trends that have attracted much attention and have been highly valued by marketers for their effectiveness.

Building Online Communities is a Must

According to Brandwatch, 90% of marketers believe that building an online community is crucial for success in 2023, and over one million people have participated in brand community groups over the past three years (2020-2022), a 16% increase compared to the previous three years.

It’s clear that businesses have gradually realized that building an online community brings many benefits. It's not only a place for customers to connect and share, but also a platform for businesses to enhance interaction and strengthen customer engagement.

When a large enough online community is built, business owners can reinforce loyalty, trust, and create strong relationships with customers, paving the way for strong and sustainable brand development.

Humorous and Relatable Content Becomes a Leading Trend

According to the 2023 Social Media Marketers report by Hubspot, surveying more than 1000 marketers, content that feels relatable to customers achieved the highest profit indicators, with 16% of marketers agreeing.

A successful brand is one that knows how to tell stories and create emotional connections through content that resonates closely with customers’ experiences and pain points. This builds trust, deep empathy, and increases absolute consumer loyalty toward the brand.

Consumer data analysis by Brandwatch shows that joy is the most sought-after emotion by social media users.

Humorous content is capable of sparking laughter, stimulating customer curiosity, creating memorable experiences, and spreading the brand's message effectively, helping businesses leave a distinctive mark among many competitors.

Instagram Delivers the Highest ROI

Instagram continues to be a strong platform in marketing in 2023, opening major opportunities for brands to reach and interact with potential customers.

Despite TikTok’s growing popularity among Gen Z, Instagram is still predicted by marketers to be the best platform for reaching potential customers (23%), according to statistics from Hubspot.

Some marketing trends you can apply through Instagram include: creating attractive visual content and videos to generate positive interaction; leveraging unique features like Instagram Reels, Instagram Live, and Instagram Shopping to spread product messages and boost sales; and reposting user-generated content about the brand.

AI is Taking Over the Marketing Landscape

With the rapid development of artificial intelligence (AI), brands and marketers are leveraging these applications to enhance efficiency and optimize their marketing campaigns.

ChatGPT, Dall-E, and Canva’s Magic Write have quickly become the top three applications associated with AI in consumers' minds.

According to consumer research by Brandwatch, recent social media discussions about AI are largely focused on these applications. Users are increasingly expecting more free AI-powered products and are eager to explore the prompts that yield the best results from AI.

In addition to gathering and analyzing data from various sources and providing information, AI can also be applied in marketing campaigns as an automated customer care chatbot. This not only saves manpower costs but also brings newer and more refreshing experiences to customers.

2023 is more than halfway through, and many surprising marketing trend scenarios are still waiting for marketers to explore and develop. Investing in thorough research and breakthrough creativity in each marketing trend is the key to success for businesses, with no other formula being more optimal in today’s era.

Source: Hubspot, Brandwatch
