GOLDEN PRINCIPLES FOR RUNNING AN EFFECTIVE KOLS BOOKING CAMPAIGN

In any field, if you want to succeed, apart from making efforts and giving your best, you need to clearly understand and grasp the most fundamental principles of that work. Once you know and master these keys, everything becomes easier and your path to success will be shortened. Running a KOLs booking campaign is no exception. Below are the golden principles you need to equip yourself with to implement an effective KOLs Booking campaign.

Understand the Product Thoroughly

To do this, you need to thoroughly research and understand the brand you are working with. Surveys, measurements, and analysis using social listening tools must be carried out from the very first steps. The clearer and more consistent the goals and orientation, the easier it will be to measure and develop a suitable strategy, thereby helping to select the most optimal KOL for the brand you are working with.

Relevance Is the Most Important Factor

No matter who the person is, how famous they are, or how much they are loved—if they do not align with the brand's orientation, you should not hesitate and must boldly remove them from your potential list.
For example, if you're looking for a brand ambassador for a marketing campaign to protect wildlife, and you choose a beautiful and famous model as the ambassador, you might think the campaign will be a huge success. But you don’t know that in the past, she wore many dresses and handbags made from animal skin. The campaign is then boycotted and strongly opposed by the community. This is the consequence of insufficient research—choosing a famous person who is not aligned with the brand's orientation.
Or choosing a muscular female athlete to perform in a high-class, fashionable fashion show can also be seen as an inappropriate and insensitive choice.

Closely Manage the Content Shared by KOLs

There will be some KOLs who are quite liberal in their language and images shared on social media. As a professional agency, you need to tightly control and work clearly with KOLs from the beginning about style orientation as well as limitations in tone and the message they will share. This will help the brand stay on the right track with the initially proposed message, avoiding misdirection due to objective reasons from the KOLs that could affect the entire ongoing campaign.

Monitoring, Evaluation, and Measurement

To know whether the campaign is effective and meets the proposed KPIs, you need to monitor each step of the campaign, then measure and evaluate using your company’s own tools or practices. This is the final step to close the campaign, helping assess performance and draw lessons for future projects.
These are the most important principles to grasp when you want to run a successful KOLs booking campaign. Hopefully, this article will provide you with useful knowledge and suggestions for your campaign.
