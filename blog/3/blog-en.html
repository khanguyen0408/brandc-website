THE HIDDEN SIDES OF THE KOL PROFESSION THAT FEW KNOW ABOUT

Being a KOL (Key Opinion Leader) is no longer unfamiliar in today's society. Many sources claim that it’s possible to easily earn hundreds of millions of VND per month through this career. Because of this, more and more people are rushing to become KOLs. But is this truly a dream job worth pursuing and investing in?

What Does a KOL Do?

In short, KOLs are individuals who have influence on social media and are famous in a specific area of expertise.
There are three main groups of KOLs:
  • Celebrities: These are famous individuals in entertainment and arts. They enjoy the highest recognition and are heavily covered by the media.
  • Influencers: Those who have significant influence and operate mainly on social media. They can come from any profession and don’t necessarily need to be experts in a particular field. Although they might not be as famous as celebrities, if their content is truly quality, their influence can spread globally.
  • Mass Seeders: Although having less influence than the other two groups, they still have a dedicated following with high engagement, particularly beneficial to small brands. Mass seeders often share and review content from KOLs and Influencers.
The scope of a KOL's work is vast and diverse, including being a brand ambassador, spokesperson, advertising actor, model, writer, and product PR specialist. With their influence and expertise, KOLs help brands change the way they reach and deliver messages to consumers.

The Glamorous Side of Being a KOL

1. Always Dressed to Impress

Due to the nature of the job, KOLs must always appear polished in front of the media. Wherever they are, they must ensure an attractive, charming appearance.
Working as a KOL, you’ll often wear luxurious clothing provided by brands for promotional purposes. Your image will always appear glamorous in public eyes, and your fame will continue to grow thanks to your beauty and style.

2. Impressive Income

A KOL’s income varies depending on their influence and popularity.
According to some sources, KOLs with a large following and extensive reach can earn around 3000 USD just for attending an event for a few hours. Additional income streams like photoshoots, commercials, and PR articles can push monthly earnings into the hundreds of millions of VND.

3. Flexible Working Hours

Unlike office workers with 8-hour shifts, KOLs have the flexibility to manage their time and work as they see fit. Freelance KOLs, in particular, can work from anywhere and collaborate on any project that suits them without being tied down by a single company.

The Hidden Challenges of Being a KOL

1. Immense Pressure from Social Media

Social media is a double-edged sword for KOLs. It can bring them wealth but can also destroy their reputation overnight.
Being a public figure, no matter the scale, means maintaining a good image and being cautious with words and actions. A single misstep or inappropriate behavior can lead to harsh criticism and being "canceled" by the public, erasing everything they’ve built. A recent example is the case of Nờ Ô Nô, which serves as a valuable lesson for the KOL community in Vietnam.

2. Numerous Temptations

Fame inevitably comes with temptations. KOLs are often approached with indecent offers, opportunities tied to illegal activities, or contracts promoting low-quality, harmful products.
Without vigilance, KOLs can be easily swayed by money, leading them down the wrong path and away from social ethics.

3. A Harshly Competitive Market

Due to the creative nature of the profession, KOLs must constantly renew their image and content quality. Updating trends and evolving while maintaining their personal brand is a constant challenge.
"The old must give way to the new" is an inevitable rule in any profession. The nature of the KOL (Key Opinion Leader) industry always demands fresh and attractive talents.
Today’s younger generation has more opportunities to engage with social media than previous ones, so their mindset and creativity are often more unique and innovative. This artistic sense naturally fits well with the current flow of the era.

Conclusion

Every career has its dark sides and pressures. To succeed in any profession, individuals must constantly strive and improve themselves.
Being a KOL is a lucrative career that can change a person's life, but it can also destroy it. If you dream of becoming a KOL, carefully research and consider all aspects of the job. With enough passion, dedication, and ethical standards, you can achieve success and a deserving position for all your efforts.
